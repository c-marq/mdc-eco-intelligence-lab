# Mentor Training

Training materials for college students who co-facilitate K-12 workshops and community events.

## Contents (Planned)

- **Facilitator guides** — One per workshop notebook, with step-by-step delivery instructions
- **Troubleshooting playbook** — Solutions for the most common student questions and technical issues
- **Facilitation techniques** — How to guide without lecturing, manage group dynamics, handle mixed skill levels
- **Assessment administration** — How to distribute and collect pre/post assessments
- **Observation checklists** — Used by faculty to evaluate mentor delivery quality

## Mentor Pathway

1. Complete the full college-level notebook sequence (`notebooks/01` through `notebooks/05`)
2. Participate in at least one faculty-led workshop as an observer
3. Co-facilitate one workshop with faculty present
4. Deliver independently with periodic faculty observation

*Materials will be populated during Phase 1–2 (2027).*
