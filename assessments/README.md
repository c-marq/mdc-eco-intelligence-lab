# Assessments

Pre/post assessments, rubrics, and scenario-based evaluations aligned with the EPA evaluation framework.

## Assessment Types (Planned)

| Assessment | Audience | Purpose |
|------------|----------|---------|
| **AI/Data Analytics Proficiency** (Pre/Post) | College students | Measure growth in data wrangling, visualization, and model interpretation skills |
| **Responsible AI Competency** (Scenario-based) | College students | 85% mastery target across four competency areas: bias, limitations, validation, ethics |
| **Project Rubrics** | College students | Evaluate culminating presentations and dashboard contributions |
| **Water Quality & AI Literacy** (Pre/Post) | K-12 students | Measure 40%+ improvement in understanding of water quality indicators and AI |
| **Community Understanding Survey** | Community members | 50% reporting increased understanding of local water quality |
| **Mentor Observation Checklist** | Faculty evaluating mentors | Quality assurance for mentor-delivered workshops |

## Development Notes

- Assessment instruments developed in collaboration with external evaluator (Dr. Jacqueline Peña)
- All assessments aligned with EPA grant evaluation requirements
- Instruments will be reviewed for cultural responsiveness and language accessibility
